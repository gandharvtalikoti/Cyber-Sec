Playfair Cipher

Playfair cipher is a manual symmetric encryption technique and was the first literal digraph substitution cipher.

Instructions:

  1. Enter a key (without spaces) in the "Enter Key" field.
  2. Enter the message in the "Enter Message" field.
  3. Click the "Encrypt" button to generate the ciphertext.
  4. Click the "Copy to Clipboard" button to copy the generated ciphertext.
